Weekly Work Plan

📊 Weekly Time Budget (≈12–15h)

  • Direct Work Search (4h): company research, tracking, outreach prep & letters.
  • Networking & Visibility (3h): two channels → general (LinkedIn, social media, Fommes.org) + technical forums (Wagtail, ImpactHub, StackOverflow).
  • Learning & Development (3–4h): improving repos, fine-tuning Unity project, adding demos/docs.
  • Speaking & Communication (2–3h): daily practice, weekly video, interview-oriented communication drills.
  • Planning, Notes, Reflection (1–2h): weekly planning, adjusting priorities, and keeping concise notes of progress.

➡️ Total: ≈12–15h/week

📅 Sample 1-Week Plan

Monday – Company Research (2h)

  • Add 3–5 companies to tracker.
  • Daily 10-min speaking practice.

Tuesday – Outreach (2h)

  • Draft/send 2 tailored letters (Engineer/Advisor).
  • Voice note: practice one letter aloud.

Wednesday – Community (2h)

  • Wagtail/ImpactHub/StackOverflow (1–1.5h).
  • Unity repo light cleanup (0.5h).
  • Voice note: summarize.

Thursday – Social Visibility (2h)

  • LinkedIn post (1h).
  • Draft Fommes.org short clip (0.5h).
  • Record 60-sec draft video (0.5h).

Friday – Repos & Learning (2–3h)

  • Unity: polish one feature.
  • Add/update README with screenshot/demo.
  • Voice note: what I learned today.

Saturday – Proof & Reflection (2h)

  • Record weekly 60–90 sec video.
  • 30-min reflection: update tracker, tweak plan.
  • 30-min notes (document week’s outputs).

Sunday – Flex & Planning (1h)

  • Light review of week.
  • Plan next 2–3 priorities.
